My projects detail

Step into a realm where music meets technology with our innovative project aimed at revolutionizing the way users interact with the Arturia MicroFreak synthesizer. Our mission is to craft a comprehensive assistance solution leveraging augmented reality technology, specifically tailored for Microsoft's HoloLens glasses. Immerse yourself in an intuitive and immersive experience as you explore the depths of the MicroFreak's capabilities. Our augmented reality solution will provide real-time visual guides and contextual information, empowering users to grasp the intricacies of the MicroFreak's features with ease. By seamlessly integrating our assistance application with HoloLens glasses, we strive to create a user interface that seamlessly aligns with the user's physical environment. This groundbreaking approach reflects our commitment to enhancing accessibility and learning for electronic music instruments. Our project goes beyond mere assistance creation; it redefines the MicroFreak usage experience by harnessing the advanced capabilities of augmented reality to make learning and exploring this synthesizer more engaging and enriching for musicians of all levels.
